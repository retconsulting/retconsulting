[BEHIND_THE_PRODUCT]

A product built end to end.

The need

Knowing what you pay each month, with no spreadsheet to maintain and no account to create. The brief fit in one sentence: see everything, be warned in time.

The choices

100% offline rather than a server: simpler, more reliable, and privacy is settled by design. No account, no ads, free.
