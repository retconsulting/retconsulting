I'm the senior profile you bring in to get a product over the line: framing the need, running the delivery, putting generative AI to work and training your teams. One person, four services, a single point of contact from kickoff to handover.

Since 2018 I've shipped products across technology, life sciences, international rail, the public sector and professional services: large, complex systems with many stakeholders to bring together.

Generative AI is my sharpest tool. I build agentic workflows into real delivery, automate the repetitive parts, and train teams on all of it: from multinationals to small crews, from first prompts to flows that non-coders run on their own.

I also ship products of my own: dueday, from first sketch to App Store release, and this site, designed and built by hand. Working end to end keeps my judgement sharp: understand the need, settle the priorities, test, document, ship.
