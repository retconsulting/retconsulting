Privacy

This site is deliberately minimal. It sets no cookies, runs no analytics and embeds no trackers or third-party scripts. Nothing about your visit is stored in your browser, and the fonts are served from this site, so your browser makes no request to any outside company as you read.

The contact form sends nothing on its own. It opens a pre-filled message in your own email app, which you then choose to send or not. When you do write to me, I use your address and message only to reply, and keep them no longer than I need to.

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc.). Like any web host, GitHub may log technical data such as your IP address and browser in order to serve the pages and keep them secure. That processing is covered by GitHub's privacy statement.

For any question about your data, or to exercise your rights under the GDPR, write to retbaz@retconsulting.be. You may also lodge a complaint with the Belgian Data Protection Authority.
